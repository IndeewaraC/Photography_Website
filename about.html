My Story

My journey into photography began over 8 years ago when I became a mother myself. I quickly realized how precious and fleeting those early moments are, and how important it is to capture them beautifully. What started as photographing my own children evolved into a passion for helping other families preserve their most treasured memories.

I specialize in family, newborn, and milestone photography because I believe these are the moments that matter most. My approach is relaxed, fun, and focused on creating a comfortable environment where genuine emotions and personalities shine through. I'm not interested in stiff, posed photos – I want to capture the real you, the laughter, the tender moments, and the beautiful chaos that makes your family unique.

Based in the Greater Metro Area, I've had the privilege of working with hundreds of families, each with their own story to tell. Whether it's a newborn's first week, a child's birthday celebration, or a family gathering, I'm here to help you preserve these precious moments in beautiful, timeless photographs.

My Photography Approach

Capturing Authentic Moments

I believe the best photographs happen when people are being themselves. That's why I focus on creating a relaxed, comfortable environment where natural interactions can unfold. Instead of forcing poses, I guide you through activities and prompts that bring out genuine smiles, laughter, and connections.

Working with Children

Having photographed hundreds of children, I've learned that patience and flexibility are key. I always come prepared with games, songs, and activities to keep little ones engaged. If someone needs a snack break or a few minutes to warm up, that's perfectly fine – we work at your family's pace.

Light and Style

I'm drawn to natural light and timeless editing styles that will look beautiful for years to come. My goal is to create photographs that feel warm, inviting, and true to life while still having that professional polish you deserve.
